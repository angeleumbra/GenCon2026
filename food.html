South Street · Gen Con Block Party

Food & Drink Guide

Decide before you're in line. These are the confirmed recurring vendors at the Block Party (Wed–Sun, South Street east of Capitol). "Dozens of rotating food trucks" fill out the rest of the lineup and aren't announced until 1–2 weeks before the con.

Only 2 food vendors show up in Gen Con's exhibitor system (Sun King Brewery and Dee-Lucious Catering) — the rest of the Block Party is organized separately from the dealer hall. The vendors below are the 8 named "Official Sponsor" fixtures confirmed on Gen Con's own Block Party page, so they should be there all weekend. Check gencon.com/gen-con-indy/block-party again closer to July 30 for the full rotating truck roster.

Before you go

  • Location: South Street, both sides, east of Capitol Ave — walking distance from ICC.
  • Days: Wednesday through Sunday during Gen Con (July 29–Aug 2, 2026).
  • Rotating trucks: the full daily lineup (dozens of trucks) isn't set until 1–2 weeks before the con, and truck order isn't decided until the morning of. Re-check the official page the week before you leave.
  • Menu detail gaps: several vendors (HotBox specific toppings, Daniel's Vineyard wine list, Dee-Lucious pricing) only publish a PDF or in-person board — links are above if you want to dig further before the trip.
